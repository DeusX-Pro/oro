our history

Crafting structures that last a lifetime

Orogold was founded with a clear vision which is to deliver quality construction and professional services built on skill, structure and trust. What started as a focus on solid building works has grown into a multi-service company handling residential construction, finishing works, HVAC installation and event management.

Over the years, we have worked on homes, estates, technical installations and event space across Delta State. Each project strengthened our commitment to proper execution, clean finishing and dependable delivery.

Today, Orogold continues to grow by staying true to its core values: craftsmanship, discipline and attention to detail. Our history is defined not by volume, but by the quality of work we leave behind and the confidence our clients place in us.
